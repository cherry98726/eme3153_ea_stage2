White Mussaenda

(Rubiaceae)

White Mussaenda is a climbing, vine-like shrub belonging to the family Rubiaceae and the genus Mussaenda. The branches and branchlets are brown, and the leaves are thinly papery, lanceolate-elliptic, with sparse soft hairs on both surfaces. The inflorescences are terminal, forming cymes, and the flowers are yellow. The flowering period extends from May to October. In some flowers, one sepal enlarges into a leaf-like, white bract.
