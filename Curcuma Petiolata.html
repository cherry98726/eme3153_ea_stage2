Curcuma Petiolata

(Zingiberaceae)

The Curcuma Petiolata reaches a height of approximately 70 to 120 centimeters. Its underground rhizomes are well developed. It is regarded as an extraordinary ornamental species, with layered bracts and refined, elegant coloration that create a striking appearance. It is a perennial herbaceous plant with rhizomatous, bulb-like growth.

The native range of the Curcuma Petiolata tulip is not yet fully certain, but it is generally agreed that it originates from the broader region of Southeast Asia.
